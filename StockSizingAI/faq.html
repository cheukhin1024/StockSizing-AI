Frequently Asked Questions

What is StockSizing AI?
StockSizing AI is an advanced portfolio optimization platform that uses artificial intelligence to help investors create well-balanced investment portfolios. Our system analyzes historical market data and uses modern portfolio theory to suggest optimal asset allocations that maximize returns while managing risk.
How do I get started with StockSizing AI?
Getting started is easy:
  1. Choose a subscription plan that fits your needs
  2. Select the assets you want to include in your portfolio
  3. Set your desired time period for analysis
  4. Click "Optimize Portfolio" to receive your personalized portfolio allocation
What types of assets can I include in my portfolio?
You can include various types of publicly traded assets, including:
  • Stocks (using ticker symbols)
  • ETFs
  • Mutual Funds
  • REITs
Simply enter the ticker symbol for each asset you want to include in your portfolio.
How many assets can I analyze at once?
You can analyze between 2 and 30 assets at once. We recommend starting with 5-10 assets for optimal diversification. This limit ensures that our optimization algorithm can provide accurate and meaningful results while maintaining computational efficiency.
How often should I rebalance my portfolio?
We recommend reviewing and rebalancing your portfolio quarterly or semi-annually. However, the optimal frequency depends on several factors:
  • Market conditions and volatility
  • Your investment strategy
  • Transaction costs
  • Tax implications
Premium subscribers receive notifications when portfolio rebalancing might be beneficial.
What data do you use for analysis?
We use high-quality financial data from reliable sources, including major stock exchanges and financial data providers. Our system analyzes:
  • Historical price data
  • Volume information
  • Volatility patterns
  • Asset correlations
The data is regularly updated to ensure accuracy in our analysis.
Is my data secure?
Yes, we take security very seriously. We implement multiple layers of protection:
  • Industry-standard encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure storage of user information
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • No storage of sensitive financial information
We only use the asset symbols and date ranges you provide for optimization calculations.
What are the subscription plans and pricing?
We offer several subscription tiers to meet different needs:
  • Free Plan: Basic portfolio optimization with limited features
  • Standard Plan: Advanced optimization tools and detailed analysis
  • Premium Plan: Full access to all features, including real-time optimization
Visit our pricing page for detailed information about each plan.
